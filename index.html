Rules

As a member of the Tech Love community you are expected to have noble behavior, with yourself and towards others. For this we have a code of conduct that is expected to be complied with:

As members of TechLove, you must commit to participate in the community, helping to be an appropriate environment for the personal and professional growth of other members.
Being respectful and inclusive towards others, avoiding behaviors that can harm others. 
- You must be respectful in your way of speaking and writing.

- Accept constructive feedback

- Harassment of other members should be avoided

- You should always seek to work together and not individually.

- Must participate at least for each academic period once in the activities.

As members of TechLove, you must commit to participate in the community, helping to be an appropriate environment for the personal and professional growth of other members.

Therefore, harmful behaviors must be avoided at all costs, remembering that we live for others while in community and not just for ourselves.

- Avoid the use of foul language, vulgar or any way that offends the dignity of the other person.
- Do not communicate in a derogatory way, make fun of others, or make memes to people.
- You must not harass other members.
-You must not use another's private information without their consent.
- Must not show lack of interest in the community.
- Failing your classes is a serious offense, makes your immediate expulsion from the community.
